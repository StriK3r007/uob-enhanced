About Our University

A Few Words About the University

The University of Balochistan, established in October 1970 through a gubernatorial ordinance, was governed by the University of Balochistan Act 1996. In 2022, a unified Act for all public sector universities in the province was passed, which currently governs UoB. As the largest general university in the province, UoB offers higher education across Science, Arts, and Humanities.

The university has 6 faculties, 52 departments, 6 specialized centers, 57 affiliated colleges, 3 institutes, 4 campuses, and 1 law college.

Achieving Academic Excellence at UoB

At the University of Balochistan, we provide a dynamic environment that fosters academic excellence, offering a wide range of programs in various disciplines. Our students gain not just knowledge, but the skills to apply it effectively in real-world situations.

Through rigorous academic programs, research opportunities, and diverse learning experiences, UoB prepares students for professional careers and lifelong learning.

Spiritual Growth Through Education

At UoB, we believe in holistic education that nurtures both the mind and the spirit. We encourage students to engage in values-based learning, promoting moral and ethical development that transcends academic knowledge.

Our commitment to spiritual growth fosters a sense of responsibility, compassion, and integrity, empowering students to make positive contributions to society.

Skill Development for Career Success

The University of Balochistan is dedicated to equipping students with the practical skills necessary for success in the modern workforce. We offer hands-on learning opportunities, internships, and workshops designed to enhance critical thinking, communication, leadership, and technical skills.

Our graduates are prepared to tackle the challenges of their respective fields, excelling in both local and global job markets.

Fostering Personal Growth and Development

At UoB, we focus on personal development as a core part of the student experience. Through extracurricular activities, leadership programs, and counseling services, students gain valuable life skills that help them thrive both during their academic journey and beyond.

We encourage self-reflection, resilience, and emotional intelligence, helping students to become well-rounded individuals who can adapt to and succeed in diverse environments.
